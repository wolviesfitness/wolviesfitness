How It Works

THE FORMULA

01
BMR Calculation
Your Basal Metabolic Rate — the calories your body burns at complete rest. Calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, the most accurate formula for most people.
02
Activity Multiplier
Your BMR is multiplied by your activity level to get your Total Daily Energy Expenditure — the actual calories you burn each day.
03
Goal Adjustment
Fat loss: 400 calorie deficit. Muscle gain: 300 calorie surplus. Maintenance: TDEE exactly. All adjustments are conservative to preserve muscle.
04
Macro Split
Protein set at 2g/kg bodyweight. Remaining calories split 50% carbs / 25% fat — optimised for performance and body composition.
